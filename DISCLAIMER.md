## About the DisParity Team

**DisParity** is a pseudonymous, leaderless collective of independent builders.
We have no founders, no foundation, no registered entity, and no central
authority. We speak only through code and through the [MANIFESTO](MANIFESTO.md).

no privileged peer) toward *disparity* (gatekeepers, token oligarchies,
foundations that govern like states). Our work is a critique-by-construction:
