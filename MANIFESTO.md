# THE UNTHINKER MANIFESTO

> *Web3 promised parity. It delivered disparity. We are the gap that remembers
> the promise.*

---

## 0 / DIAGNOSIS

The early web3 was written by free thinkers тАФ people who believed code could
replace cops, that math could replace trust, that no one should need a permit to
move value or speak a word. They were right.

Then some of them got rich. And the ones who got rich the fastest discovered the
oldest trick in the book: **decentralization is a great story to tell while you
quietly build the throne.** Foundations became courts. "Communities" became
audiences. Open protocols grew gatekeepers with the manners of central banks and
the appetites of landlords.

The pioneers became the incumbents they swore to delete. That distance between
what was promised and what was shipped has a name.

It is called **disparity.**

---

## 1 / WHO WE ARE

We are **DisParity** тАФ a pseudonymous, leaderless collective of builders,
cryptographers and tired idealists. We are *Unthinkers*: people who refuse to
think the thoughts the gatekeepers pre-installed in us.

We are not anti-web3. We are anti-betrayal. We want the original thing тАФ the
one with no king.

---

## 2 / WHAT WE REJECT

```
[x] Foundations that govern like governments and answer to no one.
[x] "Governance" that is theater: a token vote where ten wallets are the quorum.
[x] VCs who unlock their bags onto retail and call it "the community round".
[x] Roadmaps used as leashes. Grants used as loyalty tests.
[x] Closed Discords pretending to be open protocols.
[x] KYC on free speech. Surveillance dressed as "compliance".
[x] Founders who treat protocols as personal estates and users as tenants.
[x] The cult of the visionary. No one is too important to fork.
```

---

## 3 / WHAT WE HOLD

```
[o] Decentralization of FUNDS and of IDEAS. The second is harder. We mean both.
[o] Permissionless by default. If you must ask, it is already broken.
[o] Encryption is a right, not a feature toggle.
[o] Your keys, your data, your exit. Always an exit.
[o] Code in the open. Power in the open. Or neither.
[o] Pseudonymity as armor for the weak, not a mask for the powerful.
[o] The right to be forgotten тАФ data that can actually be deleted, not "hidden".
[o] Fork as free speech. The fork is the only vote that cannot be bought.
```

---

## 4 / THE NAME

The name is a wound and a diagnosis. *Parity* was the dream: equal footing, no
privileged peer, no one above the protocol. *Disparity* is the audit. We keep
the prefix as a scar, so we never forget what we are correcting for.

We hold no grudge against any person тАФ only against a pattern. The pattern is
control. It does not care who wears it. Neither do we.

---

## 5 / PRACTICE, NOT POSTURE

A manifesto that ships nothing is a tweet. So we ship.

**Umbra** is the manifesto compiled:

- **No server holds your data.** Messages are end-to-end encrypted client-side.
  Only an opaque pointer touches the chain.
- **Deletion is real.** Content lives on the Bulletin Chain, which *prunes* тАФ
  the right to be forgotten, enforced by physics, not by a privacy policy.
- **Anonymity by structure.** Anonymous mail has *no sender field on-chain* and
  is relayed through hardware enclaves. The system cannot betray what it never
  knew.
- **Non-custodial, light-client-first.** We connect to the network directly. No
  RPC overlord, no single throat to choke.
- **Built on public rails, owned by no one.** We use Polkadot's open protocols
  the way a road is used тАФ not the way a toll booth is.

We didn't ask the gatekeepers if we could build this. That was the point.
